Monsters and Monocles PSX Announcement

December 3, 2015

Monsters and Monocles is a tale of comrades, ballistics & high tea. This co-op twin stick shooter has style for days!

Credits

Trailer by - Marlon Wiebe
Game by - Retro Dreamer
Audio by - Hyperduck Soundworks

Making Of

In order to capture the frenetic multiplayer action of a four player Monster and Monocles session, I brought my computer to the local Halifax Game Collective meetup and enlisted the help of some very willing volunteers to play the game while I recorded the session.
